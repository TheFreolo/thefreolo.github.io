Calcolo delle probabilità e statistica matematica

The following links provide material for the course I am teaching assisting that is called “Calcolo delle probabilità e statistica matematica”. In the part of the course covered in this pages, I introduce R to the students and I show some statistic applications.

The course introduces the fundamental concepts related to probability and statistics. It is part of the bachelor degree in Mathematics at the University of Trento (Italy).

The content of these pages is in Italian.
